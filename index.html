About

The HackTrain conference is a day to celebrate RailTech successes!

The HackTrain Startups

The HackTrain Startups showcasing the results from the trials they carried out in only three months! They implemented their technologies in stations, trains and depos!

Owning Group Achievements

The Train Companies sharing knowledge from the key learnings on speeding up procurement processes, and how the successfully managed to get the trials going within just a couple of weeks!

Barriers to Rail Innovation Report

The announcement of the Barriers to Rail Innovation Report! A piece that outlines what are the key points that hinder startups, and TOCs to implement new technologies in the industry!

HackTrain Legal Framework

We'll be launching the HackTrain Legal Framework! A set of legal resources acredited by 5 out of the 6 largest transpot owning groups that will enable startups and train operators perform inexpensive, low-risk trials much faster!
